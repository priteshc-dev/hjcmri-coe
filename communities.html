Inclusive and Cohesive Communities

Building inclusive and cohesive communities means ensuring that every individual, regardless of background, has access to opportunities, care, and a sense of belonging.

"Hirabai Cowasji Jehangir Medical Research Institute" (HCJMRI) is a prime example of this vision in action. Established in 1995 and located within the Jehangir Hospital premises in Pune, HCJMRI is committed to advancing scientific research and improving the quality of human life.

Recognized by the Government of India as a DSIR-approved multidisciplinary research center, and holding 80G and 35(i)(ii) certifications, HCJMRI focuses on key health areas like child nutrition, endocrine disorders, Type 1 diabetes, maternal health, and non-communicable diseases.

Through its extensive outreach program for children with Type 1 Diabetes, HCJMRI exemplifies inclusivity by offering free medical aid, education, and ongoing support to underserved families.
