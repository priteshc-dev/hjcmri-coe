Awards

At our Center of Excellence for Type 1 Diabetes, we are committed to pioneering research, outstanding patient care, and impactful community initiatives. Our awards recognize exceptional contributions and breakthroughs that advance the understanding and management of Type 1 Diabetes. Explore our achievements and join us in celebrating the relentless pursuit of excellence in diabetes care and innovation.

Received first prize in the Category
Awarded to:

A study comparing the effect and safety of once weekly dosing of somapacitan with daily Norditropin® as well as evaluating long-term safety of somapacitan in a basket study design in children with short stature either born small for gestational age or with Turner syndrome, Noonan syndrome, or idiopathic short stature. Meeting was held at Bangalore on 15th December 2024.
